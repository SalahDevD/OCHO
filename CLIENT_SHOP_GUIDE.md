## Shopping Features

### Browsing Products

**Search & Filter:**
- Use the search bar to find products by name or reference
- Filter by category (dropdown menu)
- Filter by gender (Homme, Femme, Enfant, Unisexe)
- Filters work together to narrow results

**Product Cards:**
- Shows product name, reference, and price
- Displays current stock status
- Shows availability (e.g., "âŒ Rupture de stock" if out of stock)
- Each card has action buttons

### Product Actions

**Quick Add:**
- Enter desired quantity in the input field
- Click "Ajouter" (Add) button
- Product is instantly added to cart

**View Details:**
- Click the â„¹ï¸ button to open product details modal
- See full product information
- View available sizes and colors
- Select quantity and add to cart from detail view

### Shopping Cart

**Cart Sidebar (Right Side):**
- Shows all items in your cart
- Displays quantity for each item
- Shows subtotal per item
- Remove items with "Supprimer" button

**Cart Totals:**
- Real-time subtotal calculation
- Final total amount displayed
- "Passer la commande" button to checkout

### Placing an Order

1. **Add Products** to cart using any of the above methods
2. **Review Cart** in the sidebar
3. **Click "Passer la commande"** button
4. Order is created and sent to the system
5. You'll be redirected to your **Commandes** (Orders) page
6. See confirmation of your order

## Product Details Modal

## Stock & Availability

**Stock Indicators:**
- **Green (Normal)** - More than 5 items in stock
- **Red (Low Stock)** - 5 or fewer items in stock
- **âŒ Out of Stock** - No items available (button disabled)

**Validation:**
- Cannot add more items than available stock
- Quantity input limited to available stock
- System prevents overstocking
